
Hardware Checklist
Your dedicated PC with the i7-6700K and 64GB RAM
A USB flash drive (8GB+) for the OMV installer
A small SSD or USB drive for the OS (do NOT install OMV on your data drives)
Your 2TB HDDs (at minimum 3 drives: 2 for data + 1 for parity)
Ethernet cable (wired connection strongly recommended for a server)
